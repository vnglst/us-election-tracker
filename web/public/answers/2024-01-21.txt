The latest developments in the U.S. elections include concerns among Republican party officials about turmoil within some state parties potentially impacting their performance in upcoming elections, particularly in key battleground states such as Michigan, Florida, Arizona, and Georgia. In the Republican presidential nomination race, former President Donald Trump's strong showings in Iowa and New Hampshire have left some questioning whether the race is effectively over for his competitors, Nikki Haley and Ron DeSantis, due to their unfavorable ratings among Republican voters compared to Trump's net positive rating.
